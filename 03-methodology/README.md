# 03-methodology/

**Purpose**: The DataMind Method — the repeatable, quality-driven approach to delivering intelligence solutions. The authoritative source for "how we work."

## What Belongs Here

- DataMind Methodology framework
- Service delivery lifecycle and phases
- Quality gates and assurance standards
- Technical standards and best practices
- Data governance and privacy controls
- Security requirements for engagements
- Client communication protocols
- Delivery templates and checklists
- Lessons learned and improvement standards

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- ❌ Service definitions (see 04-services/) — Describes the service; methodology describes delivery
- ❌ Company strategy (see 01-company/)
- ❌ Brand guidelines (see 02-brand/)
- ❌ Product roadmaps (see 07-products/)
- ❌ Website strategy (see 05-website/)
- ❌ Operating processes (see 08-operations/)

## Key Distinction: Methodology vs. Services

- **Methodology** ("how we work") — The process, quality gates, standards, and approach we use for any engagement
- **Services** ("what we deliver") — See 04-services/ for service definitions, tiers, and pricing

Example:
- Service: "Business Intelligence Development" (what we offer)
- Methodology: "Our approach uses semantic modeling, DAX measure libraries, and agile sprint delivery" (how we do it)

## Using Methodology Standards

**For DataMind Forge**: Enforce methodology standards when planning and tracking engagements.

**For implementation repositories** (e.g., demo/accelerator projects): Follow approved methodology when:
- Structuring code and repositories
- Building data pipelines
- Creating documentation
- Defining quality standards
